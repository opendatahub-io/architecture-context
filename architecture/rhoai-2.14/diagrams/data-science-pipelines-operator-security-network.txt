POD-TO-POD TLS:
===============
When spec.podToPodTLS=true (default for RHOAI):
  - MariaDB: Serves on TLS port 3306 with service-ca certificate
  - MLMD gRPC: Serves on TLS with service-ca certificate
  - API Server: Connects to MariaDB and MLMD over TLS
  - Certificate Management: OpenShift service-ca-operator auto-generates certificates
