CA Bundle Management
================================================================================

- Platform CA: odh-trusted-ca-bundle ConfigMap (injected by platform)
- Custom CA: User-provided via MLflow CR spec
- Init container combines system + platform + custom CAs into single PEM bundle
- Watcher sidecar monitors ConfigMap changes and regenerates bundle
