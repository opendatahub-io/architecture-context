Authentication & Authorization:
================================

TrustyAI Service (via Route):
  - Auth: OAuth Proxy (OpenShift OAuth)
  - Enforcement: oauth-proxy sidecar (port 8443)
  - Policy: OpenShift SAR check - pods/get in namespace

Operator Metrics:
  - Auth: kube-rbac-proxy
  - Enforcement: proxy-role ClusterRole
  - Policy: tokenreviews + subjectaccessreviews

Guardrails Orchestrator (via Route):
  - Auth: Edge TLS only (no application-level auth)
  - Enforcement: OpenShift Router
  - Policy: None (route-level TLS termination only)
