Authentication & Authorization:
===============================

- /metrics endpoint: Protected by kube-rbac-proxy sidecar (SubjectAccessReview)
  in default overlay; direct access in OpenShift overlay
- Notebook services: No auth at this controller level; handled by
  odh-notebook-controller (OAuth proxy injection) in RHOAI deployments
- Health/readiness probes (:8081): No authentication (internal only)
