SERVICE MESH INTEGRATION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Detection: opendatahub.io/service-mesh=true annotation on Notebook resource

Behavior:
├─ OAuth NetworkPolicy ({notebook-name}-oauth-np) is NOT created
├─ Reason: Service mesh (Istio) handles mTLS and network access control
├─ mTLS: Managed by Istio sidecar proxies (STRICT or PERMISSIVE mode)
└─ Authorization: Managed by Istio AuthorizationPolicy resources

Note: Controller still creates ctrl-np NetworkPolicy regardless of service mesh annotation.
