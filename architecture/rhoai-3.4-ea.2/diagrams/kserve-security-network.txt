  - serving.knative.dev: services, services/finalizers → full CRUD
  - route.openshift.io: routes → full CRUD
  - keda.sh: scaledobjects, scaledobjects/finalizers → full CRUD
  - opentelemetry.io: opentelemetrycollectors → full CRUD
