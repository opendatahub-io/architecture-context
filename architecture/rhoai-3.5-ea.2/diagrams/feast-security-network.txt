TLS Configuration:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

- Go operator: HTTP/2 disabled (CVE-2023-44487 mitigation)
- Go feature server: tls.Config{MinVersion: tls.VersionTLS12}, curves: P256, X25519MLKEM768
- OpenShift serving certs: Auto-provisioned via service annotation
- Custom CA bundles: config.openshift.io/inject-trusted-cabundle ConfigMap injection
- Inter-service: InsecureSkipVerify=true for operator→registry (cluster-internal only)
