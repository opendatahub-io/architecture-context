  ⚠ EPP Dockerfile does NOT use -tags strictfipsruntime

  ⚠ Self-signed certs generated as fallback (RSA 4096, 10-year validity)

    X-Encoder-Endpoints) -- SSRF risk mitigated by --enable-ssrf-protection
