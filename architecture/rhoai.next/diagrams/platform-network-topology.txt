SERVICE MESH CONFIGURATION
================================================================================

- Istio mTLS mode: STRICT (default for serving namespaces)
- PeerAuthentication: PERMISSIVE on metrics ports (e.g., caikit-tgis 8086)
- Pod-to-pod TLS: OpenShift service-ca certificates for non-mesh services
- Application-level mTLS: CodeFlare (per-cluster CA), ModelMesh (optional)
- EnvoyFilter ext_authz: kube-auth-proxy integration for Gateway auth
- Certificate rotation: Managed by Istio (mesh certs) + cert-manager (webhooks)
