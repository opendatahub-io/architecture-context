SECURITY NOTES
================================================================================

  ⚠  6 components have NO application-level authentication:
     guardrails-regex-detector, guardrails-detectors, llm-d-inference-scheduler,
     llm-d-routing-sidecar, NeMo-Guardrails, rhds-llama-stack-distribution
     → Rely entirely on NetworkPolicy for isolation

  ⚠  FIPS compliance is inconsistent:
     21 Go components: strictfipsruntime enabled
     5 Go components: FIPS disabled (spark-operator, wva, llm-d EPP, etc.)
     Python/Rust/Java: No FIPS-validated crypto
     vllm-gaudi: Explicitly removes OpenSSL FIPS provider

  ⚠  Unauthenticated operational endpoints:
     vllm-cpu: /pause, /resume, /update_weights, /sleep on serving port
     llm-d-kv-cache: InsecureSkipVerify=true for EPP metrics scraping
     odh-model-controller: Self-signed Ray TLS with 3-year validity, no rotation
