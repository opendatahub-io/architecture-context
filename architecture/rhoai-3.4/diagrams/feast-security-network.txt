TLS Configuration
================================================================================

- OpenShift: Auto-provisioned via service serving cert signer annotations
  (service.beta.openshift.io/serving-cert-secret-name)
- Non-OpenShift: User-provided TLS secrets
- CA Bundle: odh-trusted-ca-bundle ConfigMap mounted when available
- Operator Build: FIPS-compliant (CGO_ENABLED=1 GOEXPERIMENT=strictfipsruntime)
