============================================================

Option 1: Volcano Scheduler
  - CRD Group: scheduling.volcano.sh
  - CRD Kind: PodGroup
  - Purpose: Ensures all training pods scheduled together
  - Operator creates PodGroup for each job
  - Volcano scheduler enforces gang scheduling

Option 2: scheduler-plugins
  - CRD Group: scheduling.x-k8s.io
  - CRD Kind: PodGroup
  - Purpose: Alternative gang scheduling implementation
  - Uses Kubernetes scheduler-plugins

Option 3: None (default)
  - Standard Kubernetes scheduling
  - May result in partial scheduling for large jobs
  - Risk of deadlock if resources are limited
