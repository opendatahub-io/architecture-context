Service Mesh Configuration:
===========================

Note: DSPO does not use Istio Service Mesh by default. Network isolation is enforced via:
- NetworkPolicies (namespace-scoped ingress rules)
- OAuth Proxy (external authentication)
- Service Account tokens (internal authentication)
- Internal services are ClusterIP only (no external exposure without Routes)
