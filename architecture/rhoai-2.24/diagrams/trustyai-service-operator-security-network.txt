Authentication & Authorization:
===============================

- TrustyAI Service (internal :80): No auth (cluster-internal only)
- Operator Metrics (:8443): kube-rbac-proxy (TokenReview + SubjectAccessReview)
- Guardrails Orchestrator (via Route): No application-level auth (edge TLS only)
- LMEvalJob Pods: ServiceAccount token, restricted security context
