================================================================================
Network Policy Notes
================================================================================

- No network policies defined by caikit-nlp directly
- All services are ClusterIP (internal only)
- Ingress managed by ModelMesh/KServe serving layer
- TLS is configurable but not enforced at runtime level
- Security delegation model: caikit-nlp relies on platform for auth/encryption
